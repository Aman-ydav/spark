Ruhul Amin Choudhary is a passionate mentor at SPARK and a faculty member at the Innovation Studio, LPU. With his invaluable guidance, he supports SPARK in every aspect, helping students shape ideas into meaningful projects. His expertise in event management, strategic planning, and motivational speaking makes him an exceptional leader who inspires and empowers young innovators. Always approachable and encouraging, he plays a key role in fostering a culture of creativity and collaboration, ensuring SPARK continues to thrive as a hub for innovation and student-driven success.

Mick is an internationally experienced entrepreneur and marketing expert, specializing in developing innovative, sustainable solutions across various industries. With over 25 years of experience, he has supported organizations, governments, and institutions in adapting to dynamic global changes. Passionate about forming new alliances and identifying business opportunities, Mick mentors at institutions like PUM Netherlands and Startupbootcamp, leveraging his vast network to drive impactful, sustainable innovation. His work emphasizes creativity, technology, and user-centric solutions that align with global sustainability efforts.

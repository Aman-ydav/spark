Smart India Hackathon 2024

We successfully coordinated the Smart India Hackathon Finals 2024, conducted at Lovely Professional University. This nationwide event provided a platform for students to solve pressing societal challenges. It was an incredible experience to witness teamwork and innovation, as participants collaborated to develop impactful solutions. The event not only fostered creativity but also showcased the exceptional organizational skills of our team.

TechSprint 2.0

TechSprint 2.0 was a collaborative event between Lovely Professional University and DPS Jalandhar, aimed at fostering innovation and technical expertise. Our team coordinated the event, which included engaging competitions for school students. Participants showcased their talents, and the event highlighted the importance of teamwork and skill-building. It was a rewarding experience to contribute to such a successful initiative.
